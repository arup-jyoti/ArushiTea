Product Details

This is ArushiTea, an online platform that offers affordable and quality tea products at your door steps.

Assam tea, hailed as a jewel of India, embodies a robust legacy within a single cup. Its distinctive malty flavor and vibrant hue stem from the fertile plains of Assam, India's largest tea-producing region. Renowned globally, this tea variety, primarily crafted from Camellia sinensis var. assamica, defines a cultural cornerstone in Assamese traditions, from ceremonial rituals to daily indulgences. Beyond its rich taste, Assam tea sustains livelihoods and drives the region's economy. Its journey from the verdant fields to the teapot symbolizes a heritage steeped in history, resilience against challenges, and a flavorful promise of community, making it an unparalleled delight for tea enthusiasts worldwide.
